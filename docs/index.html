The importance of safety on the internet

The importance of mental well-being has become increasingly important, especially for young children, due to the emergence of the internet. According to Common Sense Media, 53% of young children have reportedly seen explicit content while browsing the internet by the age of 11. Around 15% of young children have reported being cyberbullied, according to the National Center for Education Statistics. In an ideal world, every parent would be watching over their child while they browse the internet. Online predators, cyberbullying, and explicit content can have drastic effects on children at a young age. As reported by the National Institute of Mental Health, more and more children are developing depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem because of online content.

Our Solution

Safety Pro is an application designed to prevent the toxicity of the internet from reaching people. This application blocks text on the screen that has been classified as toxic. We are dedicated to creating a solution to help the younger generation browse the internet safely, without the negative effects.

Our Mission

We aim to create a safer space on the internet where people of all ages can learn and explore without the fear of encountering explicit or harmful content. Our mission is to provide a platform that promotes positive online experiences, fostering a digital environment that prioritizes safety, well-being, and learning.
